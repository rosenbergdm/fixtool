TagField NameData TypeDescriptionDepr.Valid values
59TimeInForcechar

Specifies how long the order remains in effect. Absence of this field is interpreted as DAY.

0=

Day

1=

Good Till Cancel (GTC)

2=

At the Opening (OPG)

3=

Immediate or Cancel (IOC)

4=

Fill or Kill (FOK)

5=

Good Till Crossing (GTX)

6=

Good Till Date

[Execution Report] [Market Data-Incremental Refresh] [Market Data-Snapshot/Full Refresh] [Order - List] [Order - Single] [Order Cancel/Replace Request]
